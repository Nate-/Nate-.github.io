Volunteer

Pinole Middle School Math Club, Asst. Coach

When I was volunteering as a tutor and assistant coach for the Pinole Math Club, I helped co-found a Math 24 tournament for the 12+ club members. During the tournament, the club members competed to solve Math 24 cards, where four numbers are given and any combination basic arithmetic operations to result in 24. During that tournament, in the last minutes of each round, the hardest cards were given that they couldn’t solve in time. I decided to create this solver to help quickly provide them with an answer.
